<objective>
Quick fix: simplify the Home segmented tab control from 3 tabs (ACTIVE | DRAFTS | PAST) to 2 tabs (ACTIVE | PAST) by dropping the unwired DRAFTS tab, and add a prominent "Add an item" CTA at the top of the items list inside Registry Detail so owners viewing a specific registry have a direct path into the Add Item flow without round-tripping through the bottom-nav Add sheet's registry picker.

Purpose:
- DRAFTS tab has no clear product behaviour ("client-only derivation until v1.2 ships a real Registry.status field" per CONTEXT.md). User feedback: "I don't have a clear functionality for that." Simpler tab control keeps Home focused on what givers and owners actually use today.
- The only ways to add an item to a specific registry today are (a) bottom-nav Add sheet → registry picker (round-trip) or (b) deep links. A direct CTA on the registry the user is already viewing is the obvious affordance — it lands them on AddItemScreen pre-bound to that registry with the picker HIDDEN per the contract from quick-260428-iny.

- 2-tab Home (ACTIVE | PAST), `Registry.isDraft` deleted, `home_tab_drafts`/`home_empty_drafts` removed in both locales, `DraftHeuristicTest` deleted, StyleGuidePreview updated to drop the DRAFTS preview reference.
